Welcome to Brenda's Kids

Highlights of Brenda's Kids Educare

We provide your child to enter a stimulating environment, with qualified and professional care that offers stability and continuity to school going age and beyond.

We provide an opportunity to your child to enjoy and take part in a diversity of activities that are appropriate to his/her own development like:

  • dancing
  • singing
  • drawing
  • colouring
  • arts and craft
  • handwriting
  • communication skills and many more

What are we getting up to

Daily Activities

7:00 – 8:00; Breakfast
A nutritious meal for children who arrive early in the morning.
8:00 – 9:20; Center Free Play
Children can choose from: dress up & dramatic play; toy cars, animals; & more!
9:20 – 9:40; Morning Circle
Language ,songs, fingerplays, rhymes & other learning games to coordinate with our weekly theme.
9:40 – 10:00; Creative Art
Craft or art project to coordinate with our weekly theme.
10:00 – 10:20; AM Snack
A nutritious & healthy snack ; sometimes coordinates with our weekly theme.
10:20 – 10:40; Active Storytime
Stories where children can participate through actions, movements, and acting part of the story.
10:40 – 11:40; Creative Movement
Time to gym to get our wiggles out through tumbling, balancing, & other movement games.
11:40 – 12:00; Fine Motor Skills
Puzzles, pre-writing activities, sensory table, & other activities to focus on coordination and fine(small) muscles, also works on literacy, maths & science
12:00 – 12:30; Lunch
Healthy lunch; meal made up of at least 3 food groups.
12:30 – 14:00; Nap Time
Children lay on mattresses listening to quiet music while they rest / nap.
14:00 – 15:00; Quiet Play
As children waking up they can watch quiet educational programs, look at books, or play quietly until the rest of the children wake up.
15:00 – 15:15; PM Snack
Nutritious & fun snack; sometimes coordinates with our weekly theme.
15:15 – 18:00; Center Free Play
Dramatic play & dress up, toy cars, animals & more!
06:30-17:30; Open Hours
