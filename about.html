At Brenda's Kids

Our Mission

To supply and satisfy the needs of each and every child in the community with the life skills of numeracy, literacy, irrespective of race,creed colour, disability or religion.

To teach and assist each and every child entrusted into our care and the ability to assert themselves to the fullest potential in early development.

To guide through instructions and teaching the necessary means to overcome obstacles in learning ability, he / she may experience during the period of Early Childhood Development.

To impress on each child and every child in our care the importance of education through reading , writing and arithmetic.

To assist the child from infant to Early Childhood Development, the basics of mutual respect, love, affection, and devotion to others and the environment.

To follow the path of attitude and behaviour free of prejudice and discrimination towards others less privilege children, irrespective of race, colour, creed, religion and other disabilities.

To teach each child to have mutual respect and consideration for property of others, animals and environment and for themselves.

To show compassion and understanding to other children who are ill, handicapped and afraid of learning with other children and then to assist these children with the necessary update counselling.

Our purpose to open a ECD centre

The ECD centre has a very important role to play.

In the provision of information and resources to the family that will assist the family to meet the needs of their children.

This sharing knowledge will contribute to the development of families and communities. Inclusive Training

In the ECD Context we have a strong focus on inclusive education for all age groups

Every child has the ability to learn but the way children learn & how much knowledge they can absorb can vary specially children with learning disabilities and learning difficulties.
There are many children with special needs in schools and ECD centres. They may have a special or emotional disadvantage as a result of family circumstances, abuse or neglect.

Reason for including learning disabilities and learning difficulties children.

  • They can learn from others
  • They can learn new skills from limitation
  • Mainstream schools are in the community but special needs schools are far away
  • They develop positive attitudes that are different from them
  • They government cannot build enough special schools
